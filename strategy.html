Coping Strategies

Techniques to manage stress and support your mental wellbeing.

  • Practice mindfulness or meditation daily to calm your mind.
  • Write your thoughts in your journal to release pent-up feelings.
  • Take short breaks and spend time in nature to refresh your mind.
  • Engage in physical activity to boost mood and reduce stress.
  • Use grounding techniques: focus on your senses to ease anxiety.
  • Plan self-care activities that you enjoy regularly.
  • Challenge negative thoughts and replace them with positive affirmations.
  • Reach out to someone you trust when emotions feel heavy.
